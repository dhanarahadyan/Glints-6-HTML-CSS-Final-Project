Zero Cost For Indonesian Citizen!

For Indonesian citizens, vaccination costs are fully borne by Badan Penyelenggara Jaminan Sosial (BPJS - Indonesian health insurance) by showing BPJS membership card. For those who are not registered as members of the BPJS, you must bring the Kartu Tanda Penduduk (KTP - identity card) with a photocopy of Kartu Keluarga (KK - family card)
